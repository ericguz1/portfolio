About

I am a May 2025 Cybersecurity B.S. graduate from Marist University with a deep, hands on passion for security. Over the past four years, I have spent countless hours building and testing my own labs like writing and safely reversing custom ransomware payloads with Bash Bunny, experimenting with container escapes, deploying a network wide PiHole setup that blocks a significant amount of ads and trackers, and designing multi site Cisco networks with proper segmentation and routing. I am excited to bring this practical experience into my first full time role in penetration testing, SOC analysis, application security, or security engineering. I learn quickly, document thoroughly, and genuinely enjoy both breaking and defending systems. If you are looking for someone eager to contribute and keep growing, I would love to chat.
